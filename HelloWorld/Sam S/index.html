Dog Facts

  • The shape of a dog's face can determine life span.
  • Dogs see in black and white.
  • Dogs can learn up to about 250 gestures and commands.
  • Dog's only sweat glands are between their paw pads.
  • Basset hounds cannot swim.
  • There are around four-million dogs in the world.
  • The most popular dog in the world is the labrador.
  • The average life span for a dog is ten to fourteen years.
