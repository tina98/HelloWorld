The Ohana Fanbase!

The beloved character, Stitch, in the movie Lilo and Stitch has made a big impact on many of the viewers of his movie. Stitch, originally named Experiment 626, was illegally created in outerspace by Jumba Jookiba. When the government found out, they arrested Jumba and were going to kill Stitch. Stitch had many abilities that made him almost invincible. He escaped, flew out of the galaxy, landed on Earth, and was adopted by Lilo Pelekai. From there, Lilo taught her dog, or at least she thought he was, many moral and valuable lessons. The most important one was to never leave your family behind.
