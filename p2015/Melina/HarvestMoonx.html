Harvest Moon

Character

The orignial Harvest Moon DS playable charcter is male, while in Harvest Moon DS Cute, the playable charcter is female.

However, while the male appearance is not changeable, HMDS Cute bases your character's appearence on a series of questions answered by you.

Harvest Sprites

At the beginning of the game, you will watch the story of the fight between the Harvest Goddess and Witch Princess as the Witch Princess misuses a spell, turns the Harvest Goddess to stone, and then makes her and all of the harvest sprites disappear.

This then leaves you with the task to perform various actions in order to return all 101 Harvest Sprites and the Harvest Goddess. Below is a guide on reviving each team of Sprites and their leaders.
